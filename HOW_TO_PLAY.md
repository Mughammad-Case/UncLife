# How To Play UncLife

---

## Objective

Navigate life **month by month** and build a successful unc life. maybe.

## How To Play

Each time you press Enter, one month passes.

After 12 months:

- The year increases
- The player ages up

## Core Stats

These six numbers define your unc existence. Let any of them slip and you'll feel it.

- Age
- Money
- Health
- Discipline
- Happiness
- Reputation

---

## Time System

- 1 turn = 1 month
- 12 Months = 1 Year
- Age increases every 12 months/ 1 year
